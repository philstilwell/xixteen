XiXteen turns critical thinking into a quick daily habit: 16 objective questions across 16 useful reasoning skills, with practice that adapts as your weak spots become clearer.

For everyday thinkers: get better at claims, evidence, numbers, causes, bias, and tradeoffs without needing a textbook nearby.
